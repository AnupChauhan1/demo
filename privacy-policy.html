ADVIBE PRIVACY POLICY

ADVIBE is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to the ADVIBE Web site and governs data collection and usage. By using the ADVIBE website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

COLLECTION of YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

ADVIBE collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. ADVIBE also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by ADVIBE. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by ADVIBE for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service and to provide general statistics regarding use of the ADVIBE Web site.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through ADVIBE public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others.

USE of PERSONAL INFORMATION

ADVIBE collects and uses your personal information to operate the ADVIBE Web site and deliver the services you have requested. ADVIBE also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from ADVIBE and its affiliates. ADVIBE may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

ADVIBE does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. ADVIBE may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party.

ADVIBE does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

USE of COOKIES

ADVIBE Web site use “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the ADVIBE services or Web sites you visit.

CHANGES TO THIS STATEMENT

ADVIBE will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. ADVIBE encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how ADVIBE is protecting your information.
